Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in your home boarding service?

Our home boarding service includes 24-hour care in our family home, feeding according to your dog's regular schedule, daily walks, playtime, lots of cuddles, and updates with photos during their stay. We follow your dog's routine as closely as possible to make them feel comfortable and at home.

Why do I need to book a trial session first?

The free trial session is important for both your dog and us. It allows your dog to become familiar with our home environment and meet our resident dog, Luna, before an extended stay. It also gives us the opportunity to learn about your dog's personality, needs, and preferences to ensure we can provide the best possible care.

What do I need to bring when my dog stays with you?

Please bring your dog's food, treats, any medications they require, their bed or blanket with familiar scents, favourite toys, and a lead. Also, it's helpful to provide written details about their routine, feeding schedule, and any special instructions.

Are you insured and qualified to care for dogs?

Yes, we are fully insured for pet care services. We also have experience in canine first aid and extensive experience caring for dogs of various breeds, ages, and temperaments.

How many dogs do you take at one time?

To ensure quality care and attention for each dog, we limit the number of guest dogs we accommodate at any one time. This allows us to provide a truly personalised service and make sure all dogs get the attention they deserve.

Do you provide updates while my dog is staying with you?

Absolutely! We understand that many pet parents worry about their furry family members while away. We'll send you regular updates with photos of your dog enjoying their stay, so you can have peace of mind knowing they're happy and well-cared for.
